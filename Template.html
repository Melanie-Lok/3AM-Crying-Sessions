Description

An app designed for elderly people/technologically inept individuals that provides assistance and instructions on how to use and operate certain key parts of their phone. EG. logging in to their emails/ downloading apps/ buying online etc. The app will have written instructions and an overlay of their currently being used app. Eg. arrows and lines pointing out what to click.
